Movement history is a recurring operational requirement across payments, transfers, withdrawals, and cards.

## What a strong Cobru movements guide should answer

- which surfaces emit movements
- how to reconcile webhook state changes with movement rows
- which identifiers are stable across product areas
- how to re-run reconciliation when a webhook is missed

<Callout type="warn">
  Cobru's public movement contracts are still incomplete in this repo. Treat this page as operational guidance until endpoint-level verification is complete.
</Callout>
