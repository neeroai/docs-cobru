## Platform checklist

1. Start with [Authentication](/en/docs/authentication) to stabilize token issuance and caching.
2. Continue with [Webhooks](/en/docs/webhooks) before treating payment state as production-ready.
3. Use [Testing](/en/docs/testing) and [Production readiness](/en/docs/production-readiness) to close operational gaps.
4. Use [Build with AI](/en/docs/build-with-ai) when your team relies on coding agents or internal copilots.
5. Open the [API reference](/en/docs/api/reference) when you need exact contract detail.
